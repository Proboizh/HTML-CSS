Chandigarh

Chandigarh is a city in India that is known for its cleanliness, greenery, and safety. It is also a well-planned city with a grid-like layout of roads. Chandigarh is the capital of both the states of Haryana and Punjab. The city is located at the foothills of the Shivalik mountains. Chandigarh was designed by the famous French architect Le Corbusier.
